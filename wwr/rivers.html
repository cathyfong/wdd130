Come And Explore Our Local Rivers!

Our local town has lots of nature to provide you for your fun!

Warm River Float is a four hour drive from Rexburg with a beautiful view. It has a slow, relaxing river that you can rest and slowly float on.

Next we have Siddoway Canal River Float, which is around Monkey rock. It is a pretty rapid course, so be careful when going down the course!

Lastly, we have Porneuf River located in Lava Hot Springs. Beautiful river that is an hour away. It is a fast paced float that is about 30 minutes or shorter!
